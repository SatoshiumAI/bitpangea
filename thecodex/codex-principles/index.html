The Codex
Principles

Doctrine Before Systems

The Codex Principles preserve the foundational doctrines revealed through adopted Codices. They are not separate definitions. They are the lessons discovered while defining the world.

Principles Doctrine

Definitions establish meaning. Principles preserve understanding.

Purpose

The Codex Principles collect recurring constitutional truths discovered through the Cartographic Period.

While Codices define individual concepts, Principles preserve the broader doctrines that emerge from those definitions.

Established Principles
