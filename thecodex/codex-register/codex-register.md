# The Codex Register

**Established:** June 19, 2026
**Period:** Cartographic Period

---

## Purpose

The Codex Register records formal entries adopted into The Codex.

It exists as the permanent historical record of constitutional definitions recognized within BitPangea.

The Register does not create Codices.

The Register preserves them.

---

## Register Principle

The Codex Register does not define the world.

It records the definitions adopted into The Codex.

## Scope

The Codex Register preserves:

* Codex number
* Codex title
* Adoption date
* Historical sequence

The Register does not preserve:

* Draft concepts
* Proposed definitions
* Working discussions
* Unadopted entries

Only formally adopted Codices are recorded.

---

## Historical Role

As BitPangea evolves, The Codex Register serves as the canonical historical record of constitutional cartographic definitions.

Future generations may consult the Register to understand:

* When a definition was adopted
* The sequence of constitutional development
* The evolution of BitPangea's foundational concepts

---

## Permanence

Once a Codex is formally adopted and entered into The Codex Register, its historical entry remains part of the permanent record.

New Codices may be added.

Historical adoption records are preserved.

> Definitions establish meaning.
