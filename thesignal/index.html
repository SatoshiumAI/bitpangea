The Signal

A Record of Presence

Not every message seeks a reply. Not every record seeks interpretation. A signal is evidence that someone stood at a particular place in time and chose to leave a mark.

Signal Principle Long after intentions fade, explanations disappear, and institutions change, signals remain.

What The Signal Is

The Signal is not an announcement page, marketing channel, news feed, or public bulletin.

It exists to preserve evidence of presence: brief records left behind so future readers may know that someone was here, thinking, building, and leaving marks before the world was complete.
