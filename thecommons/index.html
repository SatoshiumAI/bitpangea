The
Commons

Questions From the Edge of a New World

The Commons is the earliest public gathering place for BitPangea.

It begins simply: a place for questions, ideas, criticism, and thoughtful exploration while the project remains intentionally early.

No forum software has been launched. No accounts exist. No public board has been opened yet.

For now, The Commons begins as a curated archive of questions and signals received through the Frontier Office.

What Belongs In The Commons

  • Questions about scarce digital geography
  • Thoughtful criticism of the BitPangea concept
  • Ideas about ownership, permanence, and governance
  • Long-term civilization and worldbuilding questions
  • Research leads, historical parallels, and technical considerations
  • Questions that may help define what should never change

What Does Not Belong

  • Token requests
  • Investment solicitations
  • Parcel reservations
  • Land sale inquiries
  • Speculative price discussions
  • Spam, promotion, or hype

No parcels exist. No allocations exist. No sales exist.

First Questions

  • Why should digital land be scarce?
  • What does it mean to own a parcel?
  • What rules should never change?
  • Can a digital world have a constitutional core?
  • What kind of civilization might emerge from finite digital geography?
