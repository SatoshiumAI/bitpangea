# The Covenant

## Foundational Covenant

### Exploratory Concept · Early Stage

The Covenant exists to clarify the current status, limitations, and intentions of BitPangea.

It serves as a public statement of restraint, ensuring that the project is understood for what it is today—and what it is not.

---

## Current Status

BitPangea is an exploratory concept focused on:

- Digital scarcity
- Ownership
- Geography
- Governance
- Long-term civilization design

At present:

- No company has been formed.
- No token has been issued.
- No parcels are available for purchase.
- No blockchain has been selected.
- No allocations, reservations, or sales exist.

---

## No Offering

Nothing associated with BitPangea constitutes:

- Investment advice
- Financial advice
- Legal advice
- An offering
- A solicitation
- A promise of future availability

References to parcels, geography, ownership, governance, maps, epochs, or digital civilization are conceptual and exploratory unless expressly stated otherwise.

---

## Experimental Status

All concepts, structures, names, timelines, designs, and technical ideas remain experimental and subject to change.

BitPangea is being developed deliberately with emphasis on:

- Clarity
- Restraint
- Long-term thinking
- Avoiding premature speculation

---

## Purpose

The Covenant exists to preserve an important distinction:

There is a difference between imagining a world and implementing one.

The Covenant records that distinction.
