The History of a Scarce Digital Supercontinent

The Chronicle records the major historical developments of BitPangea through successive periods of formation, definition, discovery, and civilization.

Earlier daily records remain preserved, but the public Chronicle now follows the larger historical structure of the world itself.

May 28, 2026 – June 14, 2026

Foundation Period

The World Is Imagined

The Foundation Period established the initial institutions, principles, records, and identity of BitPangea. During this period, the project evolved from a conceptual question into an emerging civilizational framework.

Major Milestones

  • Established BitPangea from the founding question: “What if Bitcoin had been invented not as money, but as a world?”
  • Acquired bitpangea.com, created contact@bitpangea.com, and launched the first public website.
  • Published the first public Manifesto, later evolving the project language into The Charter.
  • Created the early public world surface and established the core institutions: The World, The Atlas, The Charter, The Chronicle, The Commons, and The Frontier Office.
  • Created The Covenant as a public clarification of restraint, current status, and non-offering.
  • Established The Atlas as the cartographic center of BitPangea and preserved the principle: “Before ownership can be claimed, the world must first be mapped.”
  • Defined Genesis Parcel (Lot 0) as a permanently reserved monument to the origins of BitPangea.
  • Preserved The First Stone as the earliest public artifact of the world.
  • Created The Codex as a living record of inquiry, working concepts, unresolved questions, and deferred decisions.
  • Created The Archive as a preservation layer for knowledge, records, and future inheritance.
  • Established Transmission Records and preserved Transmission 001: What If? and Transmission 002: The Map.
  • Created The Signal and preserved the first formal signals as records of presence.
  • Created The Wall as the first dedicated inscription preserved within The Reliquary.
  • Created The Lexicon as the institutional record of language, terminology, definitions, and shared vocabulary.
  • Created The Reliquary as the preservation layer for artifacts, inscriptions, monuments, interpretations, fragments, and discovery.
  • Implemented site-wide metadata, favicon standards, visual identity systems, and long-term discoverability improvements across BitPangea’s growing institutional framework.
  • Concluded the Foundation Period with BitPangea’s core institutional structure established and preserved.

June 15, 2026 – Present

Cartographic Period

Defining the World

The Cartographic Period marks the transition from imagining BitPangea to formally defining it. The focus of this period is the establishment of foundational cartographic truths and constitutional definitions.

June 15, 2026

Codex I — The Parcel

Codex I adopted the first formal cartographic definition within BitPangea by defining the Parcel as the smallest indivisible cartographic unit of the supercontinent.

  • Adopted the first formal Codex entry of the Cartographic Period.
  • Defined the Parcel as the smallest indivisible cartographic unit of BitPangea.
  • Established that parcels exist independently of ownership, discovery, development, commerce, governance, or code.
  • Established the principles:
    • Existence Precedes Discovery
    • Revelation Through Mapping
    • Ownership Is Secondary
    • The Map Must Outlast Its Inhabitants
    • Identity Persists
  • Published Codex I — The Parcel within The Codex.
  • Established the first constitutional definition of BitPangea’s Cartographic Period.
