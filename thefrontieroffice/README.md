# The Frontier Office

## Signal Transmission

### A Scarce Digital Supercontinent

The Frontier Office serves as the primary communications point for BitPangea.

It exists as a place where questions, ideas, criticism, research, and observations can be received and preserved during the project's exploratory period.

---

## Purpose

The Frontier Office exists to facilitate communication between the world and those exploring it.

While BitPangea remains in its earliest stages, the Frontier Office serves as the public point of contact for inquiry and correspondence.

---

## Current Status

BitPangea remains an exploratory concept.

- No company has been formed.
- No parcels have been issued.
- No blockchain has been selected.
- No token exists.

The project remains intentionally early.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Questions about the concept
- Long-term worldbuilding ideas
- Governance discussions
- Digital civilization thought experiments
- Research inquiries
- Constructive criticism

---

## What Does Not Belong

- Token requests
- Investment solicitations
- Land reservations
- Speculation inquiries

No parcels exist.
No allocations exist.
No sales exist.

---

## Relationship To Other Institutions

The Frontier Office serves a different role from the other major BitPangea institutions:

- The Charter preserves principles.
- The Codex preserves inquiry.
- The Chronicle preserves history.
- The Commons preserves discussion.
- The Frontier Office preserves communication.
