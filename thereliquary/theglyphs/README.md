# The Glyphs

## Reliquary Artifact

### Glyph 001

The Glyphs are preserved artifacts originating from the Foundation Period of BitPangea.

Their purpose, meaning, and interpretation remain unresolved.

The record preserves the mark before it preserves the explanation.

---

## Artifact Record

**Record:** Glyph 001

**Location:** The Reliquary

## Preservation Principle

Some marks predate language.

Some symbols survive longer than their explanations.

The Glyphs are preserved not because they are understood, but because they exist.

---

## Relationship To The Reliquary

The Glyphs belong within The Reliquary.

Unlike records that explain themselves, reliquary artifacts may be discovered before they are understood.

Interpretation may emerge later.

Preservation comes first.

---

## Classification

Glyph 001 represents one of the earliest symbolic artifacts preserved within BitPangea.

Whether it is:

- A marker
- A monument
- A signal
- A language fragment
- A navigational symbol
- Something else entirely

remains an open question.
