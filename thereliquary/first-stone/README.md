# The First Stone

## Reliquary Record

### May 28, 2026 · Day #1

The First Stone is a preserved historical artifact documenting the earliest public appearance of BitPangea.

It exists not because the first version was complete, but because beginnings matter.

---

> Every world begins somewhere.
>
> This was ours.

---

## Purpose

The First Stone preserves a record of origin.

It serves as a reminder that every institution, map, archive, civilization, and world begins as a simple first step.

The artifact exists to preserve memory rather than perfection.

---

## Historical Significance

The First Stone marks:

- The earliest public appearance of BitPangea
- The beginning of the Foundation Era
- The first visible expression of the concept
- A preserved point of reference for future generations

As BitPangea evolves, The First Stone remains unchanged.

Its value comes not from complexity, but from chronology.

---

## Relationship To The Reliquary

The First Stone belongs within The Reliquary.

Unlike public institutions that explain the world, The Reliquary preserves artifacts that help future observers understand where the world came from.

The First Stone is one such artifact.

---

## Preservation Principle

The earliest version of a world may eventually appear primitive when viewed from the future.

That is precisely why it should be preserved.
